DevOps / Site Reliability Engineer

We are hiring a DevOps/SRE Engineer to build and maintain the infrastructure powering our cloud-native platform serving millions of users worldwide.

ROLE SUMMARY
You will work at the intersection of software engineering and operations — designing reliable, scalable, and secure infrastructure, building automation tools, and ensuring our systems achieve industry-leading availability. You will own SLOs, on-call processes, and infrastructure as code.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design, build, and maintain Kubernetes-based infrastructure across multiple cloud regions
- Build and improve CI/CD pipelines enabling fast, safe deployments for 100+ engineers
- Define and enforce SLOs/SLAs; lead incident response and blameless post-mortems
- Implement infrastructure as code using Terraform and Ansible
- Build monitoring, alerting, and observability platforms (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK)
- Automate toil through scripting in Python, Go, or Bash
- Collaborate with engineers to design reliable, observable microservices
- Implement security best practices across cloud infrastructure

REQUIRED SKILLS
- 5+ years of DevOps, SRE, or infrastructure engineering experience
- Expert level Kubernetes administration (RBAC, networking, storage, multi-cluster)
- Strong experience with Terraform for cloud infrastructure provisioning
- Hands-on experience with AWS, GCP, or Azure (at least one cloud expert-level)
- Proficiency with CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, or ArgoCD)
- Experience with monitoring stacks: Prometheus, Grafana, Alertmanager
- Strong scripting skills in Python, Go, or Bash for automation
- Linux system administration experience

PREFERRED SKILLS
- Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) or equivalent
- Experience with GitOps workflows (ArgoCD, FluxCD)
- Knowledge of service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
- Experience with chaos engineering tools (Chaos Monkey, LitmusChaos)
- AWS/GCP/Azure professional-level certifications
- Experience with eBPF-based observability tools
- Go development experience for building internal tooling

EDUCATION
- B.S. in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience
- Certifications in cloud or Kubernetes highly valued

COMPENSATION
$160,000 – $230,000 base salary + equity + benefits

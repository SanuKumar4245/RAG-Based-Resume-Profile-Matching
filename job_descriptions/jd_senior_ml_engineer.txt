Senior Machine Learning Engineer

We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI Platform team. You will design and deploy production-scale machine learning systems that power our core products.

ROLE SUMMARY
As a Senior ML Engineer, you will work across the full ML lifecycle — from data exploration and model development to deployment, monitoring, and continuous improvement. You will collaborate closely with research, data engineering, and software engineering teams.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design, train, and deploy machine learning models at scale (10M+ daily predictions)
- Build and maintain ML pipelines including data preprocessing, feature engineering, training, evaluation, and deployment
- Collaborate with data scientists and ML researchers to productionize experimental models
- Implement MLOps best practices: versioning, monitoring, CI/CD for ML, model registry
- Optimize model performance including inference speed, memory efficiency, and accuracy
- Work with LLMs, transformers, and other large-scale neural architectures
- Mentor junior ML engineers and contribute to engineering standards

REQUIRED SKILLS
- 5+ years of experience in machine learning engineering or related roles
- Strong proficiency in Python and deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow)
- Experience with MLOps tools: MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar
- Hands-on experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and ML services
- Experience deploying models to production using Docker and Kubernetes
- Solid understanding of NLP, computer vision, or recommendation systems
- Experience with data pipelines (Airflow, Spark) and feature engineering at scale
- Strong software engineering skills: code reviews, testing, CI/CD

PREFERRED SKILLS
- Experience with large language models (LLMs), RAG systems, or LangChain
- Research background or experience contributing to ML publications
- Experience with HuggingFace Transformers ecosystem
- Familiarity with model compression, quantization, or efficient inference (TensorRT, ONNX)
- Knowledge of distributed training (Horovod, FSDP, DDP)
- Experience with JAX

EDUCATION
- M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field preferred
- B.S. with exceptional experience considered

COMPENSATION
$180,000 – $250,000 base salary + equity + benefits
